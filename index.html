About

I’m at Towson studying philosophy and computer science. The philosophy side sharpens how I think about arguments, bias, and choice; the CS side is where I ship—React apps, Java projects, and small APIs. I’ve built a pantry tracker to reduce food waste, a 2D game from scratch in Java, and a Spotify chatbot so you can search and control playback by typing. I care about ethical design and building things that are useful, not just clever.
